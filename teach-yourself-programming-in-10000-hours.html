Teach Yourself Programming in 10,000 Hours

Becoming an excellent programmer is hard. It takes deliberate practice, mentorship, and availability of quality resources.

It takes thousands of hours to become en expert in a field, and programming is no exception.

I want to help people get their 10,000 hours. So I'm relaunching Growing Developers.

Growing Developers will offer discussion groups, chat rooms, hangouts, and other pieces of structure to support an online community of programmers who are constantly leveling up.

If you are interested in any way, drop me a line at zee@wecohere.com and we can level up together.
